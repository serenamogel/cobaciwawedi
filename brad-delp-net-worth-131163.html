Brad Delp Net Worth

Brad Delp was an American rock singer and songwriter born on June 12, 1951 in Peabody, MA. He was best known as the lead vocalist and commanding performer of the rock group Boston, which released the hit single "More Than a Feeling". Delp was a highly acclaimed vocalist and musician, and his work with Boston was a major influence on the rock music of the 1970s and 1980s.
