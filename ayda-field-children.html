Ayda Field Children: Meet Her 4 Children

Ayda Field Children: Meet Her 4 Children: Born on May 17, 1979, Ayda Field, officially known as Ayda Sabahat Evecan is a Turkish-American actress.

She developed a love for acting at a younger age and has been consistent throughout her career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after actresses.

Field first became known on the NBC soap opera Days of Our Lives, but has subsequently specialized in comedy roles.

She was a regular on Blue Collar TV, guest starred in the sitcom Eve and appeared as Jeannie Whatley on NBC’s Studio 60 on the Sunset Strip.

On the Fox sitcom ‘Back To You’, she played Montana Diaz Herrera and went ahead to appear in ten episodes of Season One.

Field joined the cast of a sitcom pilot titled Making It Legal (playing a lawyer named “Elise”) but ABC did not pick up the pilot.

In June 2008, she was cast as the female lead on ABC’s untitled David Kohan/Max Mutchnick comedy pilot, replacing Sarah Lafleur.

Field made her UK TV debut in the final series of Fresh Meat in 2016. In that same year, she appeared in the ITV/Netflix series Paranoid.

Also in 2016, Field joined ITV’s Loose Women as a guest panelist. She’s best known for being a regular panelist on the British television show (Loose Women).

She’s also known as a judge on the British The X Factor. She joined The X Factor UK judging panel in 2018 alongside her husband, singer Robbie Williams

In April 2023, Ayda Field and her husband, Robbie Williams made headlines when they admitted they barely ever have sex.

The former Take That singer, Robbie Williams blamed his lack of libido on coming off testosterone injections, which had been worsening his depression.

In a joint interview, the pair insisted they’re ‘on the same page’ when it comes to intimacy and are content with their lack of bedroom activity.
