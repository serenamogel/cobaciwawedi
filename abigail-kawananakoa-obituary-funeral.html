Abigail Kawananakoa Obituary, Funeral

The last princess of the Hawaiian royal family, Abigail Kawananakoa has been reported dead. She died on December 11, 2022. Her death was announced by the Executive Director of the Friends of Iolani Palace, Paula Akana.

She died in her house peacefully with her wife, Veronica Gail Worth beside her.

”Abigail will be remembered for her love of Hawaii and its people. I will miss her with all my my heart,” Veronica Gail Worth said in a statement.
