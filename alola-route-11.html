Route 11 (Japanese: １１番道路 Route 11) is a route on Alola's Ula'ula Island, connecting Malie City and Route 12.

Route description

Route 11 is a straightforward grassy route leading out of Malie City. Patches of tall grass, trees, and some bamboo can be found growing on various spots around the area. The eastern end of the route is blocked off by a barricade until the player has cleared Sophocles's trial. A Trial Guide at the route entrance will heal the player's Pokémon if talked to.
