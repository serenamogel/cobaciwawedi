Early Life and Career Beginnings

Hasan Minhaj’s journey to fame and fortune began with his passion for comedy and storytelling. After graduating from the University of California, Davis, with a degree in political science, Minhaj moved to San Francisco to pursue a career in comedy.

Stand-up Comedy

Minhaj’s stand-up comedy career took off when he won Wild 94.9’s “Best Comic Standing” competition. This win led to his appearance on NBC’s stand-up comedy show, ‘Standup for Diversity’, which eventually paved the way for his television debut.

Television Career

Minhaj’s television career began with minor roles in sitcoms like ‘Arrested Development’ and ‘Getting On’. However, his big break came in 2014 when he was hired as a correspondent on ‘The Daily Show’ with Jon Stewart.

The Daily Show

Minhaj’s tenure at ‘The Daily Show’ significantly contributed to his net worth and popularity. His insightful and humorous takes on politics and social issues resonated with audiences, making him a household name.

Patriot Act with Hasan Minhaj

In 2018, Minhaj launched his own show, ‘Patriot Act with Hasan Minhaj’, on Netflix. The show, which focused on political and social issues, was critically acclaimed and won several awards, including a Peabody and two Webby Awards.

Impact on Net Worth

The success of ‘Patriot Act’ significantly boosted Minhaj’s net worth. The show ran for six seasons, with each episode reportedly earning Minhaj a substantial sum.
