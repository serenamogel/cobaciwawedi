Elisha Cuthbert Has Confirmed Her Pregnancy

Elisha Cuthbert announced her pregnancy with her husband Dion Phaneuf’s child on the Queerified with Gigi Gorgeous & Mimi podcast on December 8, 2021.

At the time of the podcast, the actress from The Ranch stated she was a little over seven months pregnant, which means she will be in her eighth month of pregnancy in January 2022.

Still, I’m beginning to feel it now. “Those final few months are incredibly difficult, especially when you’re small like me and so [dizzy],” the actress commented, admitting that she was due to give birth in a few months.
