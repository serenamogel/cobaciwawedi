Bryn Chapman Parish - Age, Family, Bio

Australian actor most known for playing "Spider" in Netflix's Heartbreak High. He has also had roles in shows like Mr. Inbetween, where he played the character of James. Besides acting, he is also a musician in a band called Mac the Knife, playing in venues around Australia. 

Before Fame

At 8 years of age, he joined a touring theatre company in France and has been acting ever since.  

Trivia

In 2019, he graduated with an Acting Bachelor's from the Western Australian Academy of Performing Arts. He has amassed over 50,000 followers on his bryncp Instagram page. 

Family Life

He was born in Sydney, Australia. 

Associated With

He and Ayesha Madon both had roles in Heartbreak High.
