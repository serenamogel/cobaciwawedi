NBA star, Stephen Curry shares 3 kids with wife, Ayesha Curry. Know his height, stats, net worth.

Two times MVP player and the NBA winner for three times Wardell Stephen ''Steph'' Curry II  a.k.a. Stephen Curry is a worldwide famous basketball player. He is widely recognized as the point guard for the Golden State Warriors.

Stephen Curry was born on 14th March 1988, in Akron Ohio, United States of America., where another NBA star Kent Bazemore was born. Born to his American parents, Dell Curry and Sonya Curry, he is of Afro-American ethnicity and holds an American nationality. 

Married to Ayesha Alexander

The famous NBA star is living happily with his longterm partner Ayesha Alexander. After dating for a long time, Stephen and Ayesha exchanged their wedding vows on 30th July 2011. 

Since their nuptials, Stephen and his wife share three children named Riley Curry (born in 2012), Ryan Curry (born in 2015), and Canon Curry (born in 2018). 

Looking at his social media, Stephen loves to spend time with his wife and kids on different destinations regardless of his busy schedules. 

Not to mention, Stephen's younger brother Seth Curry is also a basketball player, and his sister Sydel Curry is a professional volleyball player.

Stephen loves playing golf

The legendary basketball player has an immense love for golf too. He used to play golf during high school days, and also plays with his teammate Andre Iguodala. 

In 2017, Stephen Curry took part in the Ellie Mae Classic on an unrestricted sponsor exemption. Stephen missed the first cut and scored 4-over-74 for two days. 

Stephen's business interest

The famous NBA star is also popular for his partnership with Under Armour, a company that makes sportswear, casual appears, and footwear. Before his original deal with the Nike, Stephen collaborated with Under Armour in the 2013 offseason.

Curry announced his engagement in the relaunch of Palm, a mobile companion app that combines with a primary smartphone in October 2018. 

Stephen is an investor and the leading brand ambassador for Palm, a tiny San Francisco-based company that licenses TCL Corporation's Palm branding.
