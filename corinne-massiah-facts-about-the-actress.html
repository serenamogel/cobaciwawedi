10 Facts About The Actress

Corinne Massiah fell in love with acting when she was still younger. Her first acting job came she when was just 9 years of age in the CBS TV series CSI: NY. Since then, she has landed roles in Mistresses, Pitch, Secrets & Lies, and G.I. Joe: Retaliation among others.

Even though she was passionate about acting, she also involved herself in sports in the early days of her elementary education. Currently, she is furthering her education at the University of California, Los Angeles, where she will graduate in 2025.

Check Out These 10 Amazing Facts About Corinne Massiah

1. How Old is She?

It was on the 13th March 2003, that her parents welcomed her into their family in Los Angeles, California. As regards her age, she is currently 20 years old.

Just like some of her peers and colleagues in the movie industry, she knew what she wanted to become at a tender age. She developed a keen interest in acting around 2011 when she was eight years old.

2. Her Twin Sister is A Minute Older Than Her

Interestingly, Corinne wasn’t the only one that came out of her mother’s womb on the 13th of March 2003, she was born with a twin sister; Halle. However, her twin sister arrived a minute earlier before her. Therefore, she is a minute younger than Halle.

3. What is Her Educational Background

While her acting career is gradually evolving, the actress is not ready to sacrifice her education for it. After rounding up her high school education, she furthered by gaining admission into the University of California, Los Angeles (UNCLA) in 2021. Although she hasn’t revealed what she is studying at the moment, she will be graduating in 2025.

4. She Was Involved in Sports at 6

In as much as her interest as a tender child was to become an actress, she was also passionate about sports. She started playing volleyball sometime around 2009 when she was just six years old. What remains unclear is at what point she stopped playing or what made her not pursue a career in it.

5. How Did She Get Her Start in Acting?

According to reports, the Los Angeles-born actress marked her entrance into the American entertainment scene doing commercials. When Corinne turned 9, she landed her first role on the 2004 CBS TV series CSI: NY.

The 20 year-old gained popularity when she played the role of Lucy in the ABC series Mistresses, Young Ginny in the Fox series Pitch, and Young Amanda in Secrets & Lies. Also, she has appeared in G.I. Joe: Retaliation and 9-1-1.

When the movement started, Corinne was just about Ten years of age, and now she is one of the voices advocating for the rights of black. Going through her Instagram stories, the Pitch actress shares messages of different people in the past and present who have spoken about the racial injustice and segregation that black people have faced. The 20 year-old actress earns money from her acting career. Although it is not exactly clear how she has made from acting so far, looking at the number of TV series and movies she has appeared in, it is possible that her net worth is not below $500, 000.
