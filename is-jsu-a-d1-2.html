Is JSU a d2 school?

Jacksonville State is the only school in the nation to lay claim to NCAA national titles in football, men’s basketball and baseball at the Division I or Division II levels. The Gamecocks won an NCAA Division II men’s basketball crown in 1985 before claiming back-to-back baseball crowns in 1990 and 1991.

Is JSU a HBCU?
