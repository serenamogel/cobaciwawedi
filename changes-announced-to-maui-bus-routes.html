Changes announced to Maui Bus routes | News, Sports, Jobs

Beginning Wednesday, the Maui Bus will implement changes to existing bus routes, the Maui County’s Department of Transportation has announced.

The route changes will be:

• Kahului Loop No. 5 and Kahului Reverse Loop No. 6. A bus stop will be added near the new Kahului Safeway on Pulehu Place and Ho’okele Street. Minor time changes will be made for most of the existing bus stops on both routes.

• Upcountry Islander No. 40 and Haiku Islander No. 35. Minor time changes will be made, affecting most of the existing bus stops on both routes.

• Kihei Villager No. 15 and Kaanapali Islander No. 20 continue to be suspended until further notice.

Maui Bus fares are $2 per boarding or $4 for a daily pass. Monthly passes are also available.

The revised schedule of the specific routes will be posted on the county website at mauicounty.gov/bus.

Riders are able to track their bus in real-time and get arrival predictions using the Maui Bus app, available on iOS and Android or at mauibus.org.

For more information, contact the Maui County Department of Transportation at 270-7511 or email public.transit@mauicounty.gov
