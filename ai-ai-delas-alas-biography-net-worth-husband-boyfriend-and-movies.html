Ai-Ai delas Alas Biography; Net Worth, Husband, Boyfriend And Movies

Ai-Ai delas Alas is a popular Filipina film and television actress who gained fame for her film Ang Tanging Ina, which became a blockbuster hit.

Ai-Ai delas Alas was born Martina Eileen Hernandez delas Alas-Sibayan on November 11, 1964, in  San Luis, Philippines. She is the daughter of Rosendo delas Alas and Gregoria delas Alas.

Ai-Ai delas Alas had her high school education while she was with her adopted aunty and went further to earn a mass communication degree from Far Eastern University in 1985.

Ai-Ai delas Alas worked as a sales assistant in a department store, and also performed as a stand-up comic in comedy bars around Metro Manila. Her acting career started on GMA Network back in 1991, as a host of Saturday late-night musical variety show RSVP, with Dawn Zulueta and Ariel Urieta.

Ai-Ai delas Alas Husband
