Gardevoir (EX Ruby &amp; Sapphire 7)

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Gardevoir  
サーナイト Sirnight
For more information on this Pokémon's species, see Gardevoir.

Gardevoir (Japanese: サーナイト Sirnight) is a Psychic-type Stage 2 Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the EX Ruby & Sapphire expansion.

Card text

Psy Shadow
トランスシェイド
 
Once during your turn (before your attack), you may search your deck for a Energy card and attach it to 1 of your Pokémon. Put 2 damage counters on that Pokémon. Shuffle your deck afterward. This power can't be used if Gardevoir is affected by a Special Condition.
Energy Burst
エネバースト		10×
Does 10 damage times the total amount of Energy attached to Gardevoir and the Defending Pokémon.

Release information

This card was included in the English EX Ruby & Sapphire expansion with artwork by Ken Sugimori, first released in the Japanese Expansion Pack. It was subsequently reprinted in the EX Battle Stadium Deck Kit and Gardevoir EX Master Trainer Deck as both a Holofoil and Non Holofoil card, lacking the larger Card-e border featured on early EX Series cards. It was later reprinted in the English EX Power Keepers expansion with new artwork by Midori Harada, which was later released in the Japanese World Champions Pack. An exclusive Non Holofoil version of the EX Power Keepers print was included in the English Mind Game Theme Deck.
