14 Best Dips You Can Buy, According to Professional Taste Testers

The best dips can turn a party around. Maybe the mix is meh. Maybe the conversation is bland. But if the dips slap? All of that other stuff can be ignored. Lucky for you and your party guests, the Sporked team has taste tested all manner of dips, from cheesy dips to spicy dips, thick dips to thin dips. Stock up on a few of these and your next party is sure to be a hit—or, at least, people will be happy with the snack spread. So grab the best chips for dipping and let’s talk about the best dips we’ve tasted in our many, many taste tests. 

Best Tostitos Dip

Tostitos Cheesy Enchilada DipTarget

We were absolutely floored by this dip. It’s cheesy. It’s spicy. It has whole black beans in it for texture. It’s really got it all. “I can’t believe I’d never had this before. I’ll tell you what, though: I will be having this regularly from here on out. It is definitely the best Tostitos dip on the market,” senior staff writer Jordan Myrick wrote in the Tostitos dips ranking. It’s also nachos in a jar. Just add tortilla chips.

Best Spinach Artichoke Dip

Marketside Spinach & Artichoke DipWalmart

This was the creamiest, most decadent spinach-artichoke dip we tasted in our taste test because it’s made with cream cheese. Three cheeses (mozzarella, parmesan, and asiago) take it over the top to make it a seriously delicious dip.

Best Dip Mix

If you’re looking to make your own dip (but not really put in a ton of effort), then stock up on this classic mix. Best Guacamole

Hope Foods Spicy Green Chile GuacamoleAmazon

Yes, a spicy guacamole took the top spot in our best guacamole ranking. “This guacamole is deeply flavorful, spicy, and creamy,” Sporked contributor Danny Palumbo wrote in his review. “The kick of heat and subtle garlic bite doesn’t overpower the avocado itself, which is nice and thick.” The mix of habanero peppers and green chiles offers layers of zesty flavor and heat. And the flavor is all natural—no distracting tang from citric acid. It’s a true feat of store bought guac.
