If you’re anything like me, you frequently do your shopping they day before game day. I’ve got my shopping list ready to go and will be doing some heavy duty shopping in preparation for Super Bowl Sunday. I thought I’d share some of my favorite last minute game day recipes with you. These recipes require relatively few ingredients (some only three or four!) and the prep time is minimal.

A lot of these are perfect for football but are great for other game day celebrations as well. I’ve included a bit of everything – sweet, savory, and snacks. You’ll find a lot of my favorites here that frequently make an appearance at our house.

Whoever you’re cheering for, good food will make a big difference!
