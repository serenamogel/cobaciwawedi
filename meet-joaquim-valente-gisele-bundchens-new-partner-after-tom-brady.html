Career

Joaquim and his brothers, Syllo and Eliezer, were introduced to self-defense basics by a Japanese friend in the 1920s. Raised in the jujutsu tradition, they began training under their father and received private lessons from Grandmaster Helio Gracie at the tender age of two. Furthermore, Joaquim excelled in criminology at Barry University and earned a Professor’s Diploma from Grandmaster Helio Gracie in 2007.
