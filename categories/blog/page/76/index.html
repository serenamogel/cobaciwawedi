What is bye called in South Korea?

Jan 22, 2024 “Goodbye” in Korean (standard) There are 4 ways to say “Goodbye” in Korean that use the Standard form: You can say 안녕히 가세요 (annyeonghi gaseyo), 안녕히 계세요 (annyeonghi gyeseyo), 가세요 (gaseyo), and 잘 가요 (jal gayo). Is Annyeong hello or goodbye? Annyeong (안녕) is a casual, informal way of saying "Hello". It is typically used among close friends and not people you have just met. There is no need to bow when you are greeting someone informally, though you could if you want to.
